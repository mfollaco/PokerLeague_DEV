How This Works

This page separates underlying performance from short-term variance to show who is truly driving results and who may be running hotter or colder than their baseline play would suggest.

Core Scores

  • Skill Score measures underlying performance using a weighted mix of finish quality, eliminations, and wins.
  • Luck Score reflects survival-based variance, showing who is currently running above or below expectation.
  • LRI stands for Luck Residual Index and is simply Luck Score minus Skill Score. Higher positive values suggest a player is outperforming their baseline. Lower negative values suggest the opposite.

Profiles & Signals

  • Profile describes where a player sits on the map: Grinding, Running Hot, Below Trend, or Running Cold.
  • Signal shows regression pressure relative to the league: Red Hot, Heating Up, Stable, Cooling Down, Ice Cold.
  • In plain English: this view helps separate who’s earning it from who may be getting a little help from the poker gods.
